3. The service

When you purchase a FacilityTruth report, you provide us with a contract document and a facility name. We analyze the contract text, retrieve publicly available facility data from government databases, and deliver a PDF report to your email address within 30 minutes of purchase.

Our reports include:

  • Plain-English explanation of contract clauses, with citations to specific articles and sections
  • Identification of clauses we consider red flags, watch items, or negotiating opportunities
  • Summary of Medicare facility ratings and publicly available inspection data
  • An overall risk score and a negotiation guide

Our analysis reflects patterns we have observed across many contracts and our interpretation of publicly available information. It does not constitute a legal determination that any clause is enforceable, unenforceable, or actionable under any state's laws.
