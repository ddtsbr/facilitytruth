<!-- FOUNDER STORY -->
<section class="story">
<div class="story-inner">
<div class="founder-avatar">D</div>
<div>
<div class="founder-name">David Thomas</div>
<div class="founder-role">Founder, FacilityTruth</div>
<div class="founder-bio">
<blockquote>"Over the years, I've worked closely with families navigating important financial decisions — helping them understand complex documents, uncover details that are easy to overlook, and gain a clear understanding of what they're committing to before moving forward."</blockquote>
<p>When someone close to me faced placing a parent in assisted living, I sat down with the contract the facility had handed them to sign that afternoon. What I found was striking. Uncapped rate escalation with no limit on frequency or amount. A binding arbitration waiver buried in a separate exhibit. A discharge clause so vague it gave the facility broad discretion to remove a resident with almost no notice. Provisions that would cost this family real money — in a document they were expected to sign under enormous emotional pressure, with no independent resource to help them understand it.</p>
<p>I looked for a tool that could help families the way I was helping this one. Something that explained the contract in plain English, flagged the problems, and told families what to push back on before signing. At a price any family could access. I couldn't find one. So I built it.</p>
<p>FacilityTruth is that resource. A plain-English analysis of the contract you're about to sign and an honest look at the facility's track record — so you can walk into that admissions office knowing exactly what you're agreeing to, and exactly what to ask for before you do.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</section>

<!-- METHODOLOGY -->
<section class="methodology">
<div class="methodology-inner">
<div class="section-label">Our methodology</div>
<h2>How we build every report</h2>
<p class="methodology-sub">What's behind the analysis — in plain English.</p>
<div class="methodology-body">
<p>Our contract analysis framework was developed through review of hundreds of real assisted living contracts — including agreements from the largest national chains, regional operators, and independent facilities across our ten covered states. The framework identifies specific clause patterns, language structures, and missing provisions that consistently appear in contracts that create problems for families.</p>
<p>Every flagged clause is cited by its exact article and section number so you can find and verify it in your own contract. We intentionally show you the source language alongside our plain-English explanation — <strong>because we want you to be able to check our work.</strong></p>
<p>Facility data comes from two primary sources. Medicare's Care Compare database — maintained by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services — provides star ratings, staffing ratios, quality measures, and federal penalty history for every Medicare-certified facility in the country. State health department inspection records provide citation history at the state level, which our system retrieves and summarizes in plain English.</p>
<p>We update our analysis framework regularly as new contract patterns emerge and as regulatory changes affect what families should watch for. The Sunrise Senior Living class action settlement in December 2024 — $18.2 million, driven by staffing representation claims — is one example of how industry litigation directly informs what we flag.</p>
</div>

<div class="not-box">
