3. The service

When you purchase a FacilityTruth report, you provide a contract document and a facility name. We process the contract through our automated system, retrieve publicly available facility data from government databases, and deliver a PDF report to your email address within 30 minutes of payment confirmation in most cases.

Our reports include:

  • A contract overview describing what the contract is and what it commits each party to
  • A clause-by-clause description of significant provisions, with citations to the article and section in your contract
  • Industry context for each described clause
  • Federal Medicare facility data where available
  • State inspection citation summaries where available
  • Questions you may want to consider asking the facility
  • Questions you may want to consider asking a qualified elder law attorney
  • Resources for finding qualified elder law attorneys and other consumer protections

Our reports describe contract language and summarize publicly available data. They do not constitute a legal determination that any clause is enforceable, unenforceable, preempted, or actionable under any state's laws.
